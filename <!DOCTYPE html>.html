About This Collection

This website is dedicated to preserving and sharing the beautiful tradition of Aarti - devotional hymns that are sung in praise of Hindu deities. The collection includes aartis for various gods and goddesses, along with their meanings and significance.

Feel free to use these aartis for your personal prayers and devotional practices.
